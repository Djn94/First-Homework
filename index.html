About Me

Born and raised in Houston, Texas, I have been performing on various instruments (most notably bass guitar and contrabass bugle) since I was a young child. Although musical studies soaked up much of my time, I did manage to find time for a few extra curricular hobbies, such as dogs, weight lifting and computers.

My early years were marked by my creeping slowly through basic HTML texts, trying to teach myself how the internet worked. Although music does still possess a quantity of my life, the time to branch out in to other interests has come, and coding is what I have picked. Affinities for typing and troubleshooting have always been a skill I feel I have wasted..
